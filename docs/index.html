NightShift

Turn off your monitors while AI agents work overnight

NightShift keeps your machine awake while letting your monitors sleep. Perfect for running AI agents, long builds, or batch jobs overnight without screen burn or wasted energy.

Windows (.exe) macOS (.command) Linux (.sh)

Features

  • Prevents system sleep while monitors stay off
  • Perfect for overnight AI agent sessions
  • No screen burn or wasted energy from idle monitors
  • One-click setup, no configuration needed
  • Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
